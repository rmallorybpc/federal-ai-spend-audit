Audit

The discipline behind the result, stated honestly. This is a descriptive audit, not a pre-registered study.

Why this page exists

A measurement claim is only as good as the rules behind it.

This study does not claim a fixed design set before the data. The rule was built and corrected while looking at results. The honest record of that work is below. The integrity here comes from transparency and sensitivity, not from pre-registration.

Not pre-registered

The rule changed after seeing data, on purpose

The first pull used two phrases and returned 1,600 awards. On review, that net was too narrow, so the rule was widened to nine phrases and the count rose to 1,979. That is a definition change made after seeing a result. It is recorded, not hidden, and both numbers are reported.

Not yet verified

The MathWorks read

That MathWorks awards are tooling is inferred from the vendor and the keyword match, not confirmed award by award. It is the most likely read, and it is flagged as not yet checked line by line.

Sensitivity is the audit

Show the number move, do not hide it

In place of a frozen design, this study reports how the number moves when the definition moves, and it separates real year-over-year growth from the one-time lift the wider lexicon adds. A reader can change the rule in one file and watch the count change. The disagreement is the evidence.
